About Me

Who am I?

Not growing up in a Christian home, I was unaware of the everlasting impact Jesus Christ could have on my life. I have a passion to teach people to be leaders and not be ashamed of who God created them to be. I am of the belief that my life is ministry; I aspire to disciple and demonstrate the love of God through my interactions with the ones around me. When I go out to eat with my wife, playing recreational sports or working out at the gym, I am constantly thinking of how the Lord is trying to reach someone. I have a desire for people to know who their creator is and how much He truly desires and loves them!

My Vision

I want people to:

  • Know the Word of God
  • Walk in purpose
  • Live out the Great Commission
  • Actively disciple others

I am passionate about seeing people come into the knowledge of who Jesus is. Knowing that the Bible is Infallible, Inerrant and Inspired by God, it is my desire that people would have an intimate understanding and passion for scripture. In a world where so many people have opinions, we as believers have an opportunity to infiltrate this world with the truth that all of creations heart longs for. While others may see the continued destruction of our world, I see it as an opportunity for the gospel to expand at a rapid pace.
